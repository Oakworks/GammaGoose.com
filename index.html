Welcome to GammaGoose – The Smart Way to Enroll Students

Simplify Class Enrollment for Schools, Districts, and After-School Programs

GammaGoose makes it easier than ever to create, manage, and enroll students in classes. Whether you're a large school district, a small independent after-school program, or anything in between, we’ve got you covered.

What is GammaGoose?

GammaGoose is an intuitive, user-friendly platform designed to streamline class enrollment. Parents can easily browse and enroll their children in classes, while educational providers can manage offerings, track student enrollment, and communicate seamlessly – all in one place.

Our platform is designed to be flexible and adaptable to your needs. Whether you're a school district, an after-school program, or a community education provider, GammaGoose can help you save time, reduce administrative burden, and improve the overall enrollment experience for parents and students.

Why Choose GammaGoose?

For Large School Districts

GammaGoose can handle large volumes of students, simplifying the enrollment process across multiple schools. Create courses, set schedules, and manage registrations effortlessly.

For Independent After-School Programs

Running an after-school program? GammaGoose lets you set up class offerings, manage registrations, and track attendance with ease.

Flexible & Scalable

From single classes to entire districts, our platform scales with your needs. Whether it’s a seasonal after-school workshop or a year-round academic offering, GammaGoose adapts to your requirements.

GammaGoose Features

Effortless Course Creation

Quickly set up new classes, from elementary to advanced levels, with all the details parents and students need to know.

Easy Enrollment for Parents/Students

Parents/Students can browse available courses, view schedules, and enroll with just a few clicks – no paperwork required!

Comprehensive Reporting & Tracking

GammaGoose offers robust reporting tools so you can track enrollments, student progress, and much more in real time.

Secure & Compliant

We prioritize security and compliance with educational regulations, ensuring all data is handled securely and responsibly.

How GammaGoose Works

Create Your Classes

Set up courses with all the necessary details: class times, descriptions, and instructor information.

Publish & Share

Once your classes are set up, parents/students can browse through your offerings and select the right fit for them.

Track Progress

Easily view who's enrolled, manage waiting lists, and communicate with parents/students directly through the platform.

Seamless Payment & Registration

If your organization charges for courses, parents/students can pay for and complete enrollment online with no hassle.

Auto Class Scheduler

Automatically schedule classes while skipping holidays, specific days of the week or month, and custom time frames like spring break or other school breaks.
